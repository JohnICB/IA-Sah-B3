Chess Artificial Intelligence

About

Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on a chessboard, a checkered gameboard with 64 squares arranged in an 8×8 grid. Using arficial intelligence , we want you to learn how to play chess from a computer because it's faster and more accurate then a human , help you to learn from best players in the world and analyzing their movements , play chess using our application an find out in real time what are the best moves you can do to win everytime.

Tutorial

Want to play chess? Don't know how? Don't worry. This section will help you.
